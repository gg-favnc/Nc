Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Nexus Command ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services. By using the Bot, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Note: The Bot is currently hosted on the owner's private server and is not shared with any third parties. The Bot is available for purchase for 102,500 Robux, at which point this policy may be updated by the new owner.

Data We Collect

1. Discord Information

When you interact with the Bot, we may collect and store:

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Server IDs where the Bot is used
  • Channel IDs where commands are executed
  • Command usage data

2. Server Data

For servers where the Bot is added, we may collect:

  • Server name and ID
  • Channel configurations
  • Role configurations
  • Custom settings for the Bot

3. Command Data

When you use commands, we may temporarily process:

  • Message content related to commands
  • User mentions and arguments
  • Command execution timestamps

4. No Sensitive Data

We do not intentionally collect:

  • Passwords or authentication tokens
  • Financial information
  • Other sensitive personal data

How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Bot's functionality
  • To improve and customize the Bot's services
  • To monitor usage and identify issues
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
  • To prevent abuse and malicious activity
  • To generate anonymous statistics

Data Retention

We retain collected data only as long as necessary to provide services:

  • Server configurations: Until the Bot is removed from the server
  • Command logs: Up to 30 days
  • Analytics data: Up to 1 year in anonymized form

Data Sharing

1. Third Parties

We do not sell or share your data with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With service providers necessary for Bot operation (e.g., hosting providers)

2. Bot Sale

In the event of the Bot being sold (currently available for 102,500 Robux):

  • All stored data may be transferred to the new owner
  • The new owner will be bound by this policy until they update it
  • Users will be notified of any ownership changes

3. Discord API

The Bot uses Discord's API and is subject to Discord's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption in transit
  • Limited access to stored data
  • Regular security reviews
  • Secure server infrastructure
Disclaimer: While we take reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

1. Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data we store
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your data

2. Exercising Your Rights

To exercise these rights:

  • Contact us through our Support page
  • Provide your Discord user ID for verification
  • Allow up to 30 days for us to process your request

3. Opting Out

You can stop data collection by:

  • Removing the Bot from your server
  • Blocking the Bot from interacting with you

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • Our Discord server announcements
  • A notification when using the Bot
  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Join our Discord server
  • Use our Support page
  • Contact Fav_Nonchalant directly on Discord
